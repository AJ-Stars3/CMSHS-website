STAFF:

The staff is made up of graduates in accordance with Ghana Education Service requirements.

GUIDANCE AND COUNSELLING:

Guidance and Councelling forms part of the services that will help the school and students to achieve their aims and solve their problems. The student is assisted to understand their abilities and interest. He/She thus attains self-insight, motivation, inspiration and wise decision-making. One is assisted to choose his/her subjects at school and pass his/her examinations. A student is also guided to develop skills for dealing successfully with the problems he/she encounters at school or after leaving school.

ADMISSION:

To gain admition, a prospective student must:
  1. Be a pupil from Junior High School(JHS).
  2. Admission to the school is through the Computerized School Selection and Placement System(CSSPS).

MEDICAL EXAMINATION:

The student is expected to go through a medical exam after being admitted. The medical examination will be conducted by the school.

DISCIPLINE:

All students are required to abide by the code of discipline as provided by the school's rules and regulation.

REPORTS:

A report on academic work and behaviour of each student will be sent to parents/guardians at the end of every semester.

PROMOTION:

Promotions are based on satisfactory classwork and terminal examinations.

SPORTS AND GAMES:

Participation in sports and games is compulsory for all students since this is essential for the physical and mental development of the student.

BOOKS:

Exercise books/Note books and Core Text books will be supplied by government. Parents are to buy the necessary Elective text books, Practical items and other learning resources to complement what government will provide for their wards.

CANTEEN:

There is a canteen which cater for the food needs of staff and students of the school.

FOOD:

  • Students are advised in their own interest to buy food prepared only under hygienic conditions in the school.
  • No food shall be eaten in the school buildings especially in the classroom other than the cafeteria.
  • Food shall only be bought during break and snack break period.
  • Students should not go out to buy food under any circumstances.

SCHOOL TIME:

Normal school reporting time is 7:00am. Assembly is at 7:30am and school closes at 4:00pm. Classes begin at 8:00am. No student is allowed to leave the school compound before 4:00pm without permission from the school authorities. Parents and guardians are therefore advised to ensure that their wards report to school in good time and should not attempt to pick them during school instructional periodd. Student who leave campus without permission constitute breaking of bounds which will attract internal punishment for first offence; subsequent offence shall attract suspension.

END OF TERM:

At the close of every term, all students must participate in the thorough cleaning of classrooms, compound, etc before departure.

HAIR DO:

  1. All girls/boys should have their hair cut short not exceeding one centimeter, evenly all round and combed nicely.

  2. No girl is allowed to perm, press, blow or have their hair chemically treated. No hair decoration, eg. ribbons, combs, clips, etc are allowed. Failure to comply will compel school authorities to send the offending student home till the student complies to the above(a & b).

MAKE UP:

No make-up such as lipstick, eye shadow or "kajikaji", nail polish etc. are allowed. Nails should always be kept short not exceeding zero point one centimeter, (0.1cm).

ORNAMENTS:

Bangles, chains, rings, beads, fanciful earringd, etc. should not be worn to the school. Only small earrings are allowed. The wearing of dark goggles is strictly prohibited unless under medical prescriptions. Failure to comply with the above will have the school confiscate the item.

FOOTWEAR:

All boys and girls should wear black shoe from Monday to Thursday. Girls will wear white socks and boys black socks. All students must be in black Playdeck footwear(sandals) on Fridays. No other fancy foot wears and slippers must be worn by students to school. Students found in any other footwear will be punished and the footwaer seized.

DRESS/UNIFORM:

No student is permitted to re-shape or adjust the uniform the school will provide him/her. In case there is the need for the above, permission should be sought from the Assistant Head Domestic/Senior Housemaster or Housemistress, before any alterations are done.

MEETING WITH WARDS DURING INSTRUCTIONAL HOURS:

Parents who wish to see their wards during school hours must report at the General Office at the school's administration block to fill a form regarding their request. Request will thence be attended to. Any parent or visitor who flouts this arrangement of the school will be sanctioned accordingly.

THINGS NEEDED AS A DAY STUDENT

  1. Cutlery set, plate, bowl and cup.
  2. Mathematical set, calculator.
  3. School bag
  4. A pair of Black leather Sandals amd Black shoes.
  5. Socks black for boys and white for girls.
  6. P.E canvas or sneakers(white)+ white T-Shirt and Shorts.
  7. Hymn Book(School Hymnal)
  8. Bible or Quran
  9. NHIS Card
  10. Lunch bag
