PUNISHMENT FOR OFFENCES

OFFENCES PUNISHMENT
1. Examination Mulpractice Cancel Paper, Suspension or Dismissal
2. Smoking/Drunkenness/Drug Abuse Suspension, Dismisal
3. Sexual Misconduct Withdrawal, Suspension
4. Incitement to rioting Suspension, Dismisal (Parents to bear the cost of damages)
5. Writing of anonymous letter Warning, Suspension, Dismissal
6. Actions bringing the school into disrepute Warning, Bonding, Counsel, Dismissal
7. Flouting the authority of the Head or other members of staff Caning, Suspension, Dismissal
8. Assault on members of staff(Teaching/Non-Teaching) Dismissal
9. Fighting Manual work, Suspension, Withdrawal
10. Assault on other students Suspension, Withdrawal
11. Pilfering or Stealing Counseling, Suspension, Dismissal
12. Possessing of India Hemp or dangerous drugs and the use of them Suspension, Dismissal
13. Terminating of pregnancy/Abortion Dismissal
14. Leaving school without permission including refusal to attend classes or official functions Manual work with counsel, Suspension
15. Failure to write examination without justifiable cause Withdrawal/Warning(and in addition be made to write the exam papers)
16. Truancy Counseling, Internal suspension, Withdrawal
17. Flouting the authority of the school prefects or seniors Counseling, Cleaning, Caning
18. Refusal to do a prescribe punishment Caning, Counseling, Suspension
19. Wearing of unauthorized articles of clothing Seizure of articles
20. Lateness to school, class etc Counseling, Cleaning, Caning
21. Intentional destruction of school's property Suspension, Dismissal(Parents to bear cost of damages)
22. Bringing mobile phone to school Seizure of phone and not returnable
23. Graffiti Student will be charged to buy a bucket of paint
24. Destruction of school's property Student will pay twice of the amount of the item destroyed
