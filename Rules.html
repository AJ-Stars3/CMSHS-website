MISCELLANEOUS RULES

  1. It is forbidden to bring radios, sound systems, cassette, TV sets, "Walkman", video, tapes, phones, ipad/ipod and any other gadgets that are not approved by the school.


  2. School furniture shall not be left on the verandah, in the open and in any unauthorized place.

  3. No student shall attend any meeting outside the school without permission from the head of the school.


  4. No student shall change his/her name without the head of the school's approval.


  5. No student is allowed to drive any motor vehicle on the school compound.


  6. Students should not smoke or drink intoxicants or take any drugs likely to affect their behaviour without the knowledge of the school authorities.


  7. Students should read notices on the Student Notice Boards daily


  8. The writing of graffiti, ie writing or drawing on walls, school furniture, floor etc. anywhere in the school is strictly forbidden.


  9. Students must not bring fireworks to the school let alone let off fireworks in the school.


  10. Students must not make noise in the administration and Teaching Blocks.


  11. Comics and magazines in which the story is told throughout in pictures and pornographic materials are banned and will be confiscated if brought to school.


  12. The chewing of gum and consuming of food or drinks, e.g ice cream, yoghurt, etc are forbidden in all classroom buildings


  13. Students must know that a breach of common sense is a breach of school rules.

Every student must not that failure to comply with any of the rules stated above(Miscellaneous Rules) will be severely punished
